Orca Money Limited – In Members’ Voluntary Liquidation


Orca Money Limited has ceased to trade and James Neill of HNH, Jefferson House, 42 Queen Street, Belfast, Co. Please note this is a solvent liquidation and all known creditors have been or will be paid in full.
